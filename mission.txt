Mission Statment:
The purpose of the Acme Proctoring Services Database is to store and maintain
information Acme uses to administer and proctor exams. 

Objectives:
Track Scheduled Tests.
Schedule tests (date, time, location)
Store student information
Store Proffessor information.
Store Proctor information.
Make seat assignments unique. 
Track available seats for paper and electronic tests. 
Create reports based on student attendance.
Create reports based on completion of tests.
Schedule proctors for exams.
Make sure every test has one proctor.
Have test uploaded to the database.
The ability to hold e-tests.
