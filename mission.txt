Mission Statment:
The purpose of the Acme Proctoring Services Database is to store and maintain
information Acme uses to administer and proctor exams. 

Objectives:
Teachers should be able to schedule tests (date, time, location) for a student.
Store Student, Teacher, Proctor, Test Center, and Test data.
Track scheduled Tests.
Track available seating for testing centers.
Create reports based on result of test (completed, incomplete, student absent, etc.).
